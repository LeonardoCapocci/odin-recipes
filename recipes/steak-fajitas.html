Steak Fajitas with Sautéed Peppers and Onions

Ingredients:

  • Thinly sliced beef steak (such as flank or skirt steak)
  • Fajita seasoning
  • Bell peppers (various colors), thinly sliced
  • Onion, thinly sliced
  • Flour tortillas
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the beef steak with fajita seasoning and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet or griddle over high heat and cook the steak slices until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  3. Remove the steak from the skillet and set it aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add sliced peppers and onions. Sauté until they are tender and slightly caramelized.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas.
  6. Serve the steak fajitas by placing the cooked steak, sautéed peppers and onions on the tortillas. Garnish with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole.
