Bacon-Wrapped Filet Mignon with Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • Filet mignon steaks
  • Bacon strips
  • Potatoes, peeled and diced
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh asparagus spears
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Wrap each filet mignon steak with a strip of bacon and secure with toothpicks.
  2. Season the steaks with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or pan-sear the steaks to your desired level of doneness.
  4. While the steaks are cooking, boil the diced potatoes until tender. Drain and mash them with butter, minced garlic, and heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss asparagus spears with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast them in the oven until tender.
  6. Serve the bacon-wrapped filet mignon with garlic mashed potatoes and roasted asparagus.
